Infrared Sensor

Product Detail

The IR Infrared Obstacle Avoidance Sensor Module is a highly efficient and adjustable proximity sensor designed for object detection in robotics and smart automation projects. Featuring a pair of infrared transmitting and receiving tubes, the module detects obstacles by reflecting emitted IR light; when an object enters the detection range, the onboard comparator circuit pulls the digital output low, triggering a visual indicator LED. It offers an adjustable detection distance ranging from approximately 2cm to 30cm via a precision onboard potentiometer, allowing users to fine-tune sensitivity for various environments. Compatible with 3.3V to 5V power supplies and easily interfaced with microcontrollers like Arduino and Raspberry Pi, this module is widely used in obstacle-avoiding robots, smart car chassis systems, and assembly line counters due to its simple digital interface and low power consumption.
