ESP32 DOIT WIFI BLUETOOTH DEVELOPMENT BOARD

Rp72.000
Product Detail

The ESP32 ESP-32 DOIT WiFi + Bluetooth IoT Development Board is a versatile and high-performance microcontroller board designed for a wide range of IoT, automation, and embedded projects. Equipped with a dual-core processor running up to 240 MHz, this board combines powerful computing capability with integrated 2.4 GHz WiFi and Bluetooth (Classic + BLE), making it suitable for both networked and standalone applications. It features 520 KB SRAM, 4 MB Flash, and extensive GPIO pins.
