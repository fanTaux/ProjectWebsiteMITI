DHT11 Temperature Sensor

Rp11.000
Product Detail

The DHT11 Temperature and Humidity Sensor is a cost-effective and reliable digital sensor module designed for basic environmental monitoring in IoT and home automation projects. Featuring a calibrated digital signal output via a proprietary single-wire protocol, it measures ambient temperature from 0°C to 50°C and relative humidity from 20% to 90% with stable performance and long-term durability. Inside, it integrates a resistive humidity sensing component and an NTC thermistor connected to a high-performance 8-bit microcontroller, ensuring fast response times and strong interference resistance. With a sampling rate of 1Hz and simple connectivity, it is incredibly easy to interface with microcontrollers without needing complex circuitry. Ideally suited for weather stations, HVAC systems, and indoor climate control, this sensor is widely supported by standard libraries for Arduino, ESP32, and Raspberry Pi, making it the go-to choice for beginners and hobbyists seeking a plug-and-play sensing solution.
